Privacy

This site ("we") uses Google Analytics and our internal webserver logs to keep track of how many people visit the site on any given day. Other than that, we DO NOT store your personal information on our servers in any way. We believe that large collections of personal information are dangerous and we have no intention of amassing one.

We will never ask you for your email address, name or Facebook account. We will never email you. We will not store cookies on your browser.

Any features on the site that persist information are storing it in your browser in its local storage. This means that we cannot restore any information if it is lost and that we do not ever collect it on our servers.
